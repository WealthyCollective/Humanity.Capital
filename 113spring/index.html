Opening anchor proposal

Chanel × 113 Spring —
catalysing the engine

Chanel × 113 Spring · Opening Season
A $1M capex investment on fitout and design — delivering a high-growth commercial engine with no further investment required.
Chanel's $1M is deployed as capex on the fitout and design of 113 Spring's two floors, catalysing an operating commercial engine that generates high-growth revenue from day one of launch. No ongoing capital requirement from either party beyond the initial fitout. 113S.vc operates, programmes and commercialises the space — Chanel receives founding anchor rights, a 50:50 revenue share and mission alignment on accessible health, female founders and community impact.
Investment structure
$1M via Chanel Foundation into our DAF as capex on fitout and design — supporting female founders, accessible health innovation and disadvantaged communities including teens and tweens.
Revenue share
50:50 revenue share across all programming, membership and events. Four revenue lines: residency, corporate, DTC launches and anchor sponsors. Transparent quarterly reporting.
What Chanel receives
Founding anchor naming rights for the season. Immersive room sponsorship. First access to portfolio brand pipeline. Editorial and podcast content. Mission alignment on women's health and accessibility.
An operating commercial engine catalysed by Chanel's capex on fitout — delivering high-growth revenue with no further investment required from either party. We operate. We programme. We fill the space. Chanel catalyses.
